HoodMapper

Welcome to HoodMapper!

HoodMapper allows you to click on a spot on the map and drop a marker. You can title that location, provide a URL for reference, and give a description. You can view your list of locations, clicking on the title to bring that marker into focus. After saving you can bookmark or copy the URL and open again later to bring open the same map and markers.
